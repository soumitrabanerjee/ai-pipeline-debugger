Design Principles

• Geometric & Clean: The logo uses precise curves and lines, no organic shapes
• Scalable: Works perfectly at any size from favicon to billboard
• Meaningful: Double helix represents both DNA (understanding root causes) and pipeline flow
• Professional: Enterprise-grade aesthetic, zero trendiness
• Accessible: Monochrome version works on any background

Usage

• Use the color version as primary (strong brand presence)
• Use monochrome on backgrounds where color conflicts
• Always maintain clear space around logo (minimum 10px)
• Never rotate, skew, or distort the logo
• When paired with text, use Inter or similar geometric sans-serif
• Tagline color: Electric Blue (#0066cc)
